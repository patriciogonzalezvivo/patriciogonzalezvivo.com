Our perception is tuned to a narrow threshold of time and space. Everything above or below that scale requires tools and artifacts to see.

This minimalist work is a real-time simulation that explores the patterns generated by planets and satellites. The astrodynamics involved produce generative compositions over time while an imaginary camera drifts through the solar system.

Together with LUNA, this work is a clock, a calendar, a glimpse of the bigger picture.

Available at [FRAMED gallery](https://frm.fm/a/patricio_gonzalez_vivo/orbitas)
