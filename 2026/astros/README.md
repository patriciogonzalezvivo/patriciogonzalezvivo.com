*Astros* is a living meditation on the present sky, an interactive web piece that translates real-time celestial positions into a luminous, ever-changing image: astronomical data rendered alongside astrological symbols, shifting as the cosmos shifts.

The work bridges ancient cosmology and contemporary computation. Drawing from live orbital data, it renders the sky not as myth or memory but as a present condition, measured, translated, and momentarily held in form. What appears is not a chart but a contemplation: the cosmos as it is, right now, in this place.

Made in conversation with [Martin Bonadeo](https://martinbonadeo.art/), [Oliverio Duhalde](https://www.oliverioduhalde.com/) (sound design), and Alejandra Eusebi Polich (astrology).
