Inspired on the Christian tradition of highlighting those whose life was considered a model of service and devotion, this series honor's those whose imagination shaped our world. Those who channel the invisible, into earth. Bringing beauty, wonder and a renewed sense of possibility.

This series is not about their achievements but their legacy as shapers of reality. Awakeners of the soul, Shepherds of consciousness, guiding us beyond the superfluous somnolence of the obvious.
