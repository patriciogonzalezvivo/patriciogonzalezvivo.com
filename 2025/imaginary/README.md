Inspired on the Christian tradition of highlighting those whose life was considered a model of service and devotion calling them saints, this series honor's those whose imagination shaped our world for better. Those who channel the divine, the transcendent, into earth. Bringing beauty and wonder to our imperfect world.

This series is not just about their achievements but their legacy. One anchor on the power of imagination and the responsibility of creators to keep shaping this world beyond the superfluous somnolence of the obvious.
