Inspired on the Christian tradition of highlighting those whose life were model of service and love. Modeling the bar of what’s the best of us.

This series search to honor those whose imagination shape our world for better. Those of us who channel the divine, the transcendent and brought it back to earth. Those whose brought back beauty and wonder, to an imperfect world. Those in power of their imagination and emotional discipline shape a better reality.

This series is not just about their achievements but their legacy. One about the power of imagination and the responsibility of creators to keep shaping this world beyond the superfluous somnolence of the obvious.
